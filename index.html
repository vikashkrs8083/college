About Us

Dr. I.S.R.J.D. College, Parihar

One among the premier colleges among B.R.A.Bihar University, Muzaffarpur, and Dr. Indal Singh Ram Janki Degree College has wide reputation for its quality of learning and a holistic approach towards grooming the students. This college is first choice of students for higher education.

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University, formerly Bihar University, popularly known as Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Bihar University, is a public university located in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India. Established in 1960, the university has 35+ constituent colleges and 100+ affiliated colleges.
